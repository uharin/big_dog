Community

Detska Metcha (Child's Dream)

The Big Dog Detska Mechta Project is designed for neighborhood children to help other local children in need. Simple wooden figures are embellished by a child, displayed at Big Dog and then purchased by you. All proceeds of a Detska Mechta Project dog go to one of our youth partners. Check Chatter to see who is benefiting now.

Coffee Table

The Big Dog Coffee Table is a few incredible locales who want to help inspire change and community in the South Side. We get together each month to discuss issues, challenge philosophy or listen to a community need. We are a working group, working to build a stronger, safer and more welcoming neighborhood.
