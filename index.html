About

Big Dog Coffee believes in the strength of community. In our experience, we have seen that coffee can bring people together - and so we have made it our mission to create a relaxing, fun, and fresh environment where people can come and share the coffees (and teas!) they adore with the people they love. So, please stop by and say hello, grab a cup of your favorite beverage, and enjoy one Pittsburgh's most inviting coffee shops!
